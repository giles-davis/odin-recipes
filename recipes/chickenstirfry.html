Chicken Stir-Fry

Great tasting stir fry, tender chicken and really nice flavor.

This chicken stir-fry recipe is packed with veggies and is quick and easy to prepare. Try adding bean sprouts, bamboo shoots, snap peas, or any of your favorite vegetables. Serve it with white or brown rice, or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 jar Dolmio sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • 1 large brown onion
  • 1 red capsicum

Method:

  1. Chop onions, mushrooms, capsicum
  2. Sautee chopped veges
  3. Cook off mince
  4. Add veges, sauce, tomato paste
  5. Simmer
